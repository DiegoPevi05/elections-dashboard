President and vice-presidents
President

•He is the head of the Executive Power, the Armed Forces and the National Police of Peru.

Vice-presidents

•They replace the president in circumstances determined by the Constitution.

Congressmen

•They represent the public, that is, they listen to their concerns and proposals.

•They legislate, which is to propose and approve, through debate, laws that benefit the country.

•They supervise or exercise political control over the Government and the public administration in general.

Andean Parliament

•They propose measures on problems that affect the Andean Community in matters of education, migration, security, environment, climate change, entrepreneurship, among others.

Security and Prevention Protocols against COVID-19

The use of mask and face shield

In addition to physical distancing and hand hygiene, they are the most important measures to reduce the risk of transmission.

Maintain physical distancing

Not less than a meter and a half. Physical contact should be avoided at all times when greeting, saying goodbye, among others.

Take your blue ink pen

So you can cast your vote in the elections and prevent coronavirus infections.
